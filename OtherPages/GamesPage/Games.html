Games

Here is a collection of games that I have created using Unity. To download any of the listed games just follow the links, or you can go to my itch.io page to find them all in one place.

I am always working on and testing new ideas so check back later to see if I have added anymore to my collection.

Featured Games

  • Hyper Flight

    Hyper Flight is a bullet-hell, race against the clock, endless runner style game. Using semi-realistic flying physics, fly across the terrain dodging enemies and collecting power-ups and additional time. If the clock hits zero or you touch the ground, it is game over.

  • One Strike

    One Strike is a one on one action fighting game. Face off with your opponent, dodging, blocking, and striking your foe. It only takes one mistake to lose, or win.

  • Monster Match

    Monster Match is a match three puzzle game. However, in Monster Match some of the tiles are monsters and will fight back. Defeat these monsters while collecting items to help you in your task.

  • Bombs Away

    In Bombs Away, pirates are attacking your tower and you must defend it by firing cannon balls from a top your tower. Aim and adjust the fire power to perfectly target the enemies.
